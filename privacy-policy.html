Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how The Origin Inc. (“the Company”) collects, uses, retains, and disposes of users’ personal information while providing its services, including websites, mobile apps, software (games), and related services (“Services”). This policy is in accordance with the Korean Personal Information Protection Act and global standards.

Article 1 (Processing of Personal Information)

The Company collects and uses personal information to provide, maintain, and improve Services. Personal data is retained as needed and may remain after service discontinuation to meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and prevent fraud.

Article 2 (Collected Information, Methods, and Purpose)

Only the minimum required information is collected, including:

  • Game Services: Account sync info (Required)
  • Customer Support: Email, phone number (Required), Name/nickname (Optional)

Automatically collected data includes device info (model, OS, MAC, IMEI), game usage logs, payment records, etc.

Article 3 (Retention Period)

Personal data is retained while users use the Services, and deleted when no longer needed. Exceptions apply for legal compliance or unresolved issues.

Log TypeRetention Period
Contracts/payments/supply5 years
Consumer complaints3 years
Usage logs (Comm. Secrets Act)3 months

Article 4 (Disposal of Personal Information)

After its use, personal data is moved to a separate DB and destroyed securely: digital files via technical methods, paper via shredding/incineration.

Article 5 (Inactive Accounts)

Accounts inactive for 1 year are flagged for disposal. Users are notified 30 days prior with details of retention expiration and data destruction.

Article 6 (Outsourcing and Overseas Transfer)

Some personal information may be handled by overseas processors for service delivery. The Company ensures proper safeguards and consent mechanisms.

Article 7 (Provision to Third Parties)

Data is shared only for service provision, payment processing, with consent, or as required by law.

Article 8 (Minors)

Children under 13 (or 16 in the EU) require parental consent. If collected, such data will be deleted upon notice.

Article 9 (Nevada Privacy Rights)

Nevada residents may request not to sell their data by contacting the Company.

Article 10 (CCPA)

California users have rights to request disclosure, deletion, and prevent sale of personal data. The Company does not sell user data.

Article 11–12 (GDPR)

Personal data is processed lawfully under GDPR based on consent, contract necessity, legal obligations, vital interests, or public interest.

Article 13 (User Rights)

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their data via help@plgames.co.kr. Withdrawal of consent results in deletion of related information.

Article 14 (Cookies)

Cookies are used for personalized services. Users can allow or block cookies via browser settings.

Article 15 (Security Measures)

  • Admin: Internal policies, employee training
  • Technical: Access control, encryption, security software
  • Physical: Data room access restrictions

Article 16 (Policy Changes)

Changes due to law or internal policy will be notified appropriately.

Article 17 (Contact)

For privacy-related inquiries, contact the Privacy Compliance Officer at help@plgames.co.kr
